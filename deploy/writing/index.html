The Desert Lamp

From 2008 to 2011, I was a co-founder and blogger at the Desert Lamp, an independent student news blog designed to make student media more competitive and student government more accountable. In addition to in-depth coverage of campus policy and politics, I organized advocacy projects to directly influence policy. We won a $10,000 prize and were named the best libertarian college bloggers in the US. You can see all of my posts here, but these are a few of my favorites:
