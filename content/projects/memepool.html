--- extends: project.j2 default_block: content_body heading: Meme Pool title: Meme Pool description: > An image blog that evolves. created: !!timestamp '2012-03-18 01:25:20' tags: - programming github: https://github.com/ecmendenhall/Meme-Pool listable: true --- ![Meme Pool]({{ media_url('images/memepool.png') }}) __[Project](http://meme-pool.tumblr.com)__ | __[Source](https://github.com/ecmendenhall/Meme-Pool)__ __Tools & Skills:__ Python, Tumblr API, Flickr API Evolution creates complex things from just three simple rules: selection, replication, and variation. I made Meme Pool as a small experiment to explore this idea. It's a Tumblr blog powered by a simple Python script that uses a genetic algorithm to "evolve" new images to post each day. I wrote it to learn a little bit about JSON and to practice interacting with the Tumblr and Flickr APIs. It runs automatically a few times each day as a cron job on Google App Engine. It's not related to the [other](http://memepool.com/), much more famous memepool. Memepool was featured in [ReadWriteWeb].
